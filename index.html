Hyunwoo Song

Postdoctoral Fellow, Johns Hopkins University

Johns Hopkins University

Baltimore, MD, USA

hsong37 [at] jhu.edu

Hello! I am a postdoctoral fellow at Johns Hopkins University, where I work with Drs. Emad Boctor, Jeeun Kang, and Russell H. Taylor. My research focuses on developing non-contact ultrasound imaging systems for surgical guidance, and photoacoustic retinal prosthesis technology.

I am actively involved in the ARPA-H and SURPASS projects, bridging ultrasound, photoacoustics, and medical robotics to enable precise, functional, and non-contact clinical intervention.
